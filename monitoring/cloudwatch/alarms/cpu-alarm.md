# CPU Utilization Alarm

## Purpose

Detect abnormal CPU utilization on compute resources.

---

## Metric

AWS/EC2

CPUUtilization

---

## Threshold

Greater than 80%

---

## Evaluation

- 2 Evaluation Periods
- 5 Minute Period

---

## Future Actions

- Amazon SNS Notification
- Lambda Auto Remediation
- Incident Ticket Creation
